n mathematics, the absolute value or modulus of a real number x,
denoted |x|, is the non-negative value of x without regard to its sign.
Namely, {\displaystyle |x|=x} if x is a positive number, and {\displaystyle |x|=-x} if x is negative, and {\displaystyle |0|=0}.
